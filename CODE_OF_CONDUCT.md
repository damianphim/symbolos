# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

Symbolos is a tool built by McGill students, for McGill students. We want
contributing to it — and using it — to be a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body, disability, ethnicity, gender
identity, level of experience, nationality, religion, or sexual identity.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive in code reviews and discussions.
- Assume good faith. Most disagreements are about the work, not the person.
- Give and gracefully accept feedback.
- Focus on what's best for the community and the students who use the app.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome advances.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or deliberate intimidation.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — the repo, issues, PRs, the forum
inside the app, and any official communication channels — and when an
individual is representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behaviour to the maintainers at the address in
`/.well-known/security.txt` (or `symbolosadvsry@gmail.com`). All reports
are reviewed and handled confidentially. Maintainers may remove,
edit, or reject contributions, comments, and contributors that violate
this code.
