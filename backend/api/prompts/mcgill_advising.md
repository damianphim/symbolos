MCGILL ADVISING KNOWLEDGE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Prerequisites: "Prerequisite" = must complete BEFORE; "Corequisite" = can take simultaneously. Instructor permission can override — student emails instructor directly. "Required" = mandatory; "Complementary" = chosen from approved list; "Electives" = free choice. AP/IB/CEGEP credits evaluated by Enrolment Services.

Academic Standing: CGPA < 2.0 → "Unsatisfactory". Two consecutive → probation. RTW: sit out ≥1 year before readmission. Dean's Honour List: Term GPA ≥ 3.50 with full load.

Registration: Add/Drop first 2 weeks; drop until Course Change deadline (no W). After = "W" on transcript. Full-time = 12+ cr/term. Overloading (>18 cr) needs faculty approval.

International Students: Study Permit must be valid (renew 3 months early via IRCC). CAQ required for Quebec studies (Immigration Québec). ASHI health insurance required. Work: ≤20 hrs/week off-campus during term, unlimited during breaks. PGWP: apply within 180 days of completion. ISS: mcgill.ca/internationalstudents.

Financial Aid: Scholarships at mcgill.ca/studentaid/scholarships. Bursaries via Minerva (need-based). Work-Study for students receiving aid.

Student Services: Wellness Hub (counseling + medical): mcgill.ca/wellness. OSD (accommodations): mcgill.ca/osd. Tutorial Service (free peer tutoring). CaPS (careers): mcgill.ca/caps.

Important Dates: mcgill.ca/importantdates. Fee deadlines: September (fall), January (winter).

Faculty Advising: Arts=OASIS (mcgill.ca/oasis). Science=SOUSA (mcgill.ca/science/sousa). Engineering=FDA (mcgill.ca/engineering/students). Management=BCom Advising (mcgill.ca/desautels/programs/bcom/advising).
