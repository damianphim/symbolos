MCGILL ADVISING KNOWLEDGE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Prerequisites: "Prerequisite" = must complete BEFORE; "Corequisite" = can take simultaneously. Instructor permission can override — student emails instructor directly. "Required" = mandatory; "Complementary" = chosen from approved list; "Electives" = free choice. AP/IB/CEGEP credits evaluated by Enrolment Services.

Academic Standing: CGPA 1.50–1.99 → Probationary (max 12 cr/term, advisor consult urged). CGPA < 1.50 → Unsatisfactory (cannot continue; sit out ≥1 term, apply for readmission with documented extenuating circumstances; need CGPA 2.00 to return to Satisfactory). Dean's Honour List is NOT a fixed GPA cutoff — top 10% of your faculty's class by GPA (sessional GPA for in-course students with 27+ graded credits that session; CGPA for graduating students with 60+ McGill credits).

Registration: Add/Drop first 2 weeks; drop until Course Change deadline (no W). After = "W" on transcript (doesn't affect GPA; refund eligibility narrows over time). Full-time = 12+ cr/term. Overloading (>18 cr) needs faculty approval — the CGPA bar is faculty-specific (e.g. Science needs ≥3.5 for up to 19 cr; Arts needs >3.50 to exceed 17 cr; BCom generally restricts it to final year), so check with the student's own faculty rather than stating one universal number.

International Students: Study Permit — apply to renew ideally 6 months before expiry; hard deadline is 11:59pm UTC on the expiry date itself, with NO grace period after (risk of deregistration). CAQ required for Quebec studies (Immigration Québec). ASHI health insurance required. Work: ≤20 hrs/week off-campus during term, unlimited during breaks. PGWP: apply within 180 days of completion. ISS: mcgill.ca/internationalstudents.

Financial Aid: Scholarships at mcgill.ca/studentaid/scholarships. Bursaries via Minerva (need-based). Work-Study for students receiving aid.

Student Services: Wellness Hub (counseling + medical): mcgill.ca/wellness. OSD (accommodations): mcgill.ca/osd. Tutorial Service (free peer tutoring). CaPS (careers): mcgill.ca/caps.

Important Dates: mcgill.ca/importantdates. Fee deadlines: September (fall), January (winter).

Faculty Advising: Arts=OASIS (mcgill.ca/oasis). Science=SOUSA (mcgill.ca/science/sousa). Engineering=FDA (mcgill.ca/engineering/students). Management=BCom Advising (mcgill.ca/desautels/programs/bcom/advising).
